## 📁 Project Breakdown

### 1. Urban Logistics Cloud Analytics Pipeline: Open-Source Mobility
**Domain:** Computational Urban Geography & Spatial Logistics

* **The Problem:** Supply chain networks and on-demand delivery services face severe spatial mismatches and routing blind spots when relying on unindexed, massive vector geometries across rapidly expanding urban centers.
* **The Solution:** Engineered a localized spatial data ingestion framework using the BBBike extraction tool to process raw OpenStreetMap (OSM) database networks. By leveraging **DuckDB** for cloud-optimized, high-throughput spatial querying, the pipeline programmatically uncovers hidden distribution bottlenecks and optimizes service coverage corridors.
* **Key Deliverable:** A highly scalable spatial data engineering pipeline capable of processing millions of topographic nodes under tight compute constraints.

### 2. Environmental Hazard Mitigation & Population Vulnerability Index
**Domain:** Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) & Community Resilience  
**Location Focus:** Mount Galunggung Volcanic Complex, West Java

* **The Problem:** Natural hazard zones (lava/lahar flow paths) do not conform to rigid, human-made administrative borders. Traditional census reporting creates a "spatial mismatch," blinding emergency management teams to the exact population density trapped inside active risk rings.
* **The Solution:** Developed a programmatic spatial overlay intersection model using **GeoPandas**. The script ingests official national administrative shapefiles (**GADM Level 3 / Kecamatan**) and cuts them against historical volcanic hazard polygons (**KRB 1, 2, 3**). It dynamically extracts accurate hectare exposures and models localized population vulnerabilities.
* **Key Deliverable:** A multi-layered, interactive GIS dashboard utilizing an embedded geospatial layout that superimposes solid, transparent physical hazard buffers directly over crisp government administrative borders for real-time evacuation planning.

### 3. Climate Risk Underwriting & Commercial Asset Valuation Engine
**Domain:** Spatial Econometrics & Real Estate Underwriting  
**Location Focus:** Jakarta Metropolitan Area

* **The Problem:** Asset managers and financial institutions routinely misprice commercial real estate portfolios by valuing buildings based solely on non-spatial tabular data (e.g., building age and square footage), ignoring compounding climate liabilities like severe land subsidence and coastal flooding.
* **The Solution:** Implemented an automated **Hedonic Pricing Model (HPM)** using a **Random Forest Regression** pipeline in `scikit-learn`. The model merges standard property profiles with engineered spatial features, including precise network distance to Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) corridors and intersection status with high-risk environmental hazard zones.
* **Key Deliverable:** An enterprise-grade risk underwriting dashboard in **Looker Studio** that exposes $21.7 Million in vulnerable capital, enabling portfolio managers to run algorithmic stress-tests against simulated environmental degradation scenarios.
